How to Play - Pawn Mode

🎮 Game Basics

Pawn Mode is the easy difficulty. Every player on your team gets +2 to all attributes, and every opponent gets -1 to all attributes. Plus, you earn 2x the training points!

⚔️ Start Your Team

Choose your identity (Attack, Defense, or Positional). Your 2 core players are guaranteed to have a 10 in that attribute. Pick 2 additional players with at least a 9 in your chosen attribute.

📋 Matches

Arrange your 4 players in formation, then select an attribute for each matchup. Your attribute is compared to the opponent's. Higher wins, ties draw. Winning gives you training points.

🏋️ Training

No risk! All training always succeeds. Spend points to boost your players. After 10 matches, rebuild your squad with new players.

📊 Progression

Complete seasons and climb the ranks. Your stats track all your achievements.

Resign from League?

This will permanently delete your squad, points, and all progress. This cannot be undone.
